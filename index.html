What is Easter?

Easter is a Christian holiday that celebrates the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead. It is observed on the first Sunday after the first full moon following the vernal equinox. Many Easter traditions, such as the Easter Bunny and egg hunts, have roots in pagan springtime celebrations.
